# Pricing Logic (价格逻辑)

## Billing Structure
Pricing is based on **session/product** count limits.

## Pricing Tiers

### 1. Free Tier
- **Price**: Free (免费)
- **Products**: 100
- **Sessions**: 100

### 2. Starter Tier
- **Price**: $19
- **Products**: 500
- **Sessions**: 1,000

### 3. Professional Tier
- **Price**: $49
- **Products**: 5,000
- **Sessions**: 4,000

### 4. Business Tier
- **Price**: $99
- **Products**: 10,000
- **Sessions**: 10,000

### 5. Enterprise Tier
- **Price**: $199
- **Features**: 
  - Custom workflow (定制workflow)
  - Unlimited products
  - Unlimited sessions

## Notes
- Each tier has defined limits for products and sessions
- Enterprise tier offers unlimited usage and custom workflow capabilities
- Pricing scales with usage needs
